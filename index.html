What is a Local Business?

Any company that provides goods or services to a local population is considered a local business. Often denoted by the phrase, "brick and mortar," a local business can be a locally owned business or a corporate business with multiple locations operating in a specific area.

Our Aim

During the past few years of the pandemic, local businesses have been suffering more than ever before. Therefore, we would like to create a website to provide these local communities a chance to thrive. We believe that we could support them by promoting and marketing their product through a safe, supportive, and inclusive platform: LOC.ALLEY
